Spinach Feta Turnover

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado, medium dice
  • 1/2 pint of grape tomatoes, quartered
  • 2 celery stalks, small dice
  • 2 tablespoons red onion, small dice
  • 2 tablespoons red or white wine vinegar, or lemon juice
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Recipe

Mix all ingredients in a bowl, smashing a few of the avocado chunks.

Salt and pepper to taste.

Serve with tortilla chips.
